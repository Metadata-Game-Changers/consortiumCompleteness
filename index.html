DataCite Consortium and Provider FAIRness

These data were collected during January 2026 and are described in
  • DataCite Bright Spots and
  • DataCite Bright Spots – Repositories, Consortia, and Improvements.

The data are available at Metadata Completeness for Four FAIR Use Cases for all DataCite Repositories.

The first image shows repository completeness distributions for sixty-six DataCite consortia. Subsequent images show the distribution of FAIRness scores for each consortium provider, with a vertical line indicating the reference score of 23%. The shaded area highlights the "bright spot" range from 40% to 63%, where providers are performing exceptionally well.
